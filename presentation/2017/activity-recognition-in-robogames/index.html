Activity recognition in a Physical Interactive RoboGame

September 22, 2017

Abstract: In this poster, I will present the ideas in our paper about a simple model to classify player’s activity in a Physically Interactive RoboGame, using a 3-axis custom accelerometer positioned on player’s chest. Please, check out the accompanying paper.
